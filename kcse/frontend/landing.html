About

The KCSE Guidance Chatbot is an AI-powered digital assistant designed to help Kenyan students navigate academic and career guidance. Get personalized advice on subject combinations, university requirements, and career paths based on your KCSE results and interests.

How It Works

1. Sign Up & Add Subjects

Create an account and input your KCSE subjects, grades, interests, and career goals for personalized advice.

2. Chat with AI

Ask questions about courses, career paths, or exam guidance. The AI uses your profile to give tailored recommendations.

3. Get Recommendations

Receive suggested courses, career options, and academic guidance based on your profile and KCSE results.

Key Features

Personalized Recommendations

Discover courses and careers that match your KCSE subjects, grades, and interests.

Expert Guidance

Get advice on subject combinations, university requirements, and career paths from AI-powered insights.

Save Your Profile

Create a profile to get ongoing personalized guidance and track your academic journey.
