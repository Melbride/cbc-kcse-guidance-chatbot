# STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) - Career Pathways

## Overview
STEM pathways offer diverse opportunities for students interested in scientific research, technological innovation, engineering solutions, and mathematical applications. This pathway leads to careers in technology, healthcare, engineering, research, and data science.

## Senior School Subject Combinations
### Core Subjects
- English Language
- Mathematics
- Kiswahili
- Physics
- Chemistry

### STEM-Specific Subjects
- **Biology** - Life sciences, medicine, biotechnology
- **Physics** - Engineering, physical sciences, technology
- **Chemistry** - Chemical engineering, medicine, research
- **Mathematics** - Data science, engineering, finance
- **Computer Science** - Software development, IT, cybersecurity
- **Geography** - Environmental science, GIS, surveying

## Career Opportunities

### Engineering and Technology
**Career Paths:**
- **Software Engineer** - Develops applications and systems
- **Civil Engineer** - Designs and constructs infrastructure
- **Mechanical Engineer** - Designs machines and mechanical systems
- **Electrical Engineer** - Works with electrical systems and power
- **Chemical Engineer** - Designs chemical processes and products
- **Environmental Engineer** - Solves environmental problems
- **Biomedical Engineer** - Develops medical devices and solutions

**Required Skills:**
- Strong mathematics and physics foundation
- Problem-solving and analytical thinking
- Technical drawing and design
- Computer programming
- Attention to detail and precision

**Training Requirements:**
- Bachelor of Engineering (5 years)
- Master's degree for specialization
- Professional registration (Engineers Board of Kenya)
- Continuous professional development

**Top Institutions:**
- University of Nairobi (School of Engineering)
- Jomo Kenyatta University (Engineering programs)
- Moi University (Engineering)
- Kenyatta University (Engineering)
- Technical University of Kenya (Engineering)

### Computer Science and IT
**Career Paths:**
- **Software Developer** - Creates computer programs and applications
- **Data Scientist** - Analyzes complex data for insights
- **Cybersecurity Specialist** - Protects computer systems and networks
- **Network Administrator** - Manages computer networks
- **Database Administrator** - Organizes and secures data
- **AI/Machine Learning Engineer** - Develops intelligent systems

**Required Skills:**
- Programming languages (Python, Java, JavaScript)
- Database management

- Problem-solving abilities
- Continuous learning mindset

**Training Requirements:**

**Institutions:**

- Zetech University

## Education and Training Pathways

### TVET Programs (1-3 years)
**Available Programs:**
- **Diploma in Information Technology** (2 years)
- **Diploma in Computer Science** (3 years)
- **Certificate in Software Development** (1 year)
- **Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology** (3 years)
- **Certificate in Networking** (1-2 years)

**Entry Requirements:**
- KCSE mean grade C- and above
- Mathematics and sciences preferred
- Technical aptitude
- Age 16-35 years

**TVET Institutions:**
- Technical Training Institutes (TTIs)
- Kenya Technical Trainers College (KTTC)
- National Polytechnics
- Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC)
- Nairobi Technical Training Institute

### University Programs (4-6 years)
**Bachelor's Degrees:**
- **Bachelor of Science in Engineering** (5 years)
- **Bachelor of Science in Computer Science** (4 years)
- **Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery** (6 years)
- **Bachelor of Science in Mathematics** (4 years)
- **Bachelor of Science in Physics** (4 years)
- **Bachelor of Science in Chemistry** (4 years)
- **Bachelor of Science in Biology** (4 years)

**Entry Requirements:**
- KCSE mean grade C+ and above
- Specific subject cluster requirements
- Strong grades in Mathematics and Sciences
- Physics and Chemistry usually required for engineering

**Cluster Points Calculation:**
- Based on best 7 subjects including required cluster subjects
- Weighted by subject importance
- Varies by university and program
- Higher cluster points increase admission chances

## Income Expectations

### Entry Level Salaries
- **Junior Software Developer**: KES 40,000-80,000/month
- **IT Support**: KES 25,000-45,000/month
- **Laboratory Technician**: KES 30,000-50,000/month
- **Engineering Trainee**: KES 35,000-70,000/month

### Mid-Level Salaries
- **Software Engineer**: KES 80,000-200,000/month
- **Data Analyst**: KES 60,000-150,000/month
- **Engineer**: KES 70,000-180,000/month
- **Medical Officer**: KES 100,000-250,000/month

### Senior Level Salaries
- **Senior Software Engineer**: KES 200,000-500,000+/month
- **Senior Engineer**: KES 150,000-400,000+/month
- **Consultant**: KES 200,000-600,000+/month
- **Specialist Doctor**: KES 300,000-1,000,000+/month

## Success Tips

### Academic Preparation
1. **Excel in Mathematics and Sciences** in high school
2. **Take additional science subjects** beyond minimum requirements
3. **Participate in science clubs and competitions**
4. **Seek mentorship from STEM professionals**
5. **Develop strong computer skills**

### Career Development
1. **Build a strong portfolio** of projects and achievements
2. **Stay updated with technology trends**
3. **Network with professionals** in your field
4. **Pursue continuous learning** and certifications
5. **Develop soft skills** - communication, leadership
6. **Consider international opportunities**

### Entrepreneurship in STEM
1. **Identify problems** that need solutions
2. **Develop technical skills** for innovation
3. **Build a professional network**
4. **Seek funding and support** for startups
5. **Learn business fundamentals**
6. **Stay current with technology trends**
