# TVET Artisan and Craft Programs - Career Pathways

## Overview
TVET artisan programs provide practical skills for immediate employment and entrepreneurship. These programs are ideal for students who prefer hands-on learning and want to enter the workforce quickly.

## Artisan Level Programs (6 months - 1 year)

### Building and Construction
- **Electrical Installation** - Installation of electrical systems in buildings
- **Plumbing and Pipe Fitting** - Water systems installation and maintenance
- **Welding and Fabrication** - Metal joining and structural work
- **Carpentry and Joinery** - Woodworking and furniture making
- **Masonry** - Bricklaying and concrete work

### Automotive and Mechanical
- **Motor Vehicle Mechanics** - Car repair and maintenance
- **Diesel Mechanics** - Heavy vehicle and equipment repair
- **Auto Electrical** - Vehicle electrical systems

### Hospitality and Service
- **Food and Beverage Service** - Restaurant and hotel service
- **Housekeeping and Accommodation** - Hotel cleaning and management
- **Culinary Arts** - Food preparation and cooking

### Business and Office
- **Secretarial Services** - Office administration and documentation
- **Accounting Clerical** - Basic bookkeeping and financial records
- **Customer Service** - Client relations and support

### Agriculture and Environment
- **Agricultural Mechanics** - Farm equipment maintenance
- **Horticulture** - Crop production and management
- **Animal Husbandry** - Livestock care and management

### Creative and Design
- **Garment Making** - Tailoring and fashion design
- **Hairdressing and Beauty Therapy** - Salon services
- **Photography** - Basic photography and image editing

## Entry Requirements
- KCSE mean grade D and below
- No strict subject requirements
- Focus on interest and aptitude
- Age: 16-25 years

## Career Opportunities
### Immediate Employment
- Construction companies
- Manufacturing firms
- Hotels and restaurants
- Garages and workshops
- Retail businesses

### Entrepreneurship
- Start own workshops
- Service businesses
- Small-scale manufacturing
- Freelance services

### Further Education
- Progress to Craft Certificate (1 year)
- Continue to Diploma (2 years)
- Specialized training programs

## Benefits of TVET Artisan Programs
1. **Quick entry to workforce** - Start earning within 6 months
2. **Practical skills** - Hands-on training for real jobs
3. **Lower cost** - More affordable than academic programs
4. **High demand** - Skilled artisans are always needed
5. **Entrepreneurship opportunities** - Start own business
6. **Progression pathways** - Can advance to higher levels

## Institutions Offering Artisan Programs
- National Industrial Training Authority (NITA) centers
- Technical Training Institutes (TTIs)
- Youth Service centers (NYS)
- Private vocational training centers
- Church-based training institutions

## Success Stories
Many successful Kenyans started with artisan programs and built successful businesses:
- Mechanics who own garages
- Electricians with contracting businesses
- Chefs who own restaurants
- Tailors with fashion houses
- Plumbers with service companies

## How to Apply
1. Visit nearest TVET institution
2. Submit KCSE results or certificate
3. Pay registration fee (varies by institution)
4. Attend orientation and begin training

## Financial Support
- Government TVET scholarships
- HELB loans for TVET students
- County government bursaries
- Institution-specific financial aid
